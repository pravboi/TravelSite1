HOW TO USE

  • Navigate to the register/login page and create an account.
  • To book a flight navigate to the schedule a trip page and select a expedition. Once inside a expedition choose a spacecraft and enter payment information to buy tickets.
  • Navigate to the order history to confirm that the transaction was succesful.
  • To complete training naviagate to the modules page and select a module. Once in a mddule answer all of the questions and click submit and you will see your score if it is high than your current high score.
